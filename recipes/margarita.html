Margarita

Description

Margarita is a simple pizza hailing from Naples. When done right, margherita pizza features a bubbly crust, crushed San Marzano tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella and basil, a drizzle of olive oil, and a sprinkle of salt.

Ingredients

  • mozzarella cheese
  • tomato sauce
  • basil

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Proof the dough
  3. Prepare the sauce and toppings
  4. Shape the dough
  5. Bake the pizza
  6. Cool the pizza
